I build software that solves real business problems—from the metal up.

My name is Vicente Garcia, and I work at the intersection of deep technical fundamentals and product strategy. I help founders and early-stage startups turn ideas into reliable, scalable technology without the overhead of a full-time CTO.

## 🧠 What I Bring

**Deep technical roots.** I learned to code by building from first principles—working directly with CPU registers in assembly, implementing core C library functions from scratch, and reconstructing network tools like `ping` to understand exactly how they work. This foundation means I don't just use technologies; I understand them.

**Product ownership.** As the solo developer at SayFly!, I took full ownership of a live Django codebase after the CTO departed. I maintained 99% uptime while shipping new features, proving that I can deliver under pressure and take complete responsibility for technical outcomes.

**Business perspective.** My four months at ESCP Business School taught me something critical: great technology is worthless if it doesn't serve a business goal. I learned to form fast, effective teams across cultural barriers and translate technical decisions into business value.

## 🛠 How I Work

I believe in simplicity, reliability, and ruthless prioritization.

When resources are tight and time is short—which is always, in a startup—I focus on building systems that are **just complex enough** to solve today's problem and **simple enough** to change tomorrow. My Go projects reflect this: clean concurrency, safe communication, and tools that do one thing well.

I'm equally comfortable debugging memory corruption in C, designing REST APIs, or talking through your business model with investors.

I'm available for fractional, interim, and advisory roles with:

- Non-technical founders who need a trusted technology partner
- Growing companies needing architectural guidance and technical leadership

Whether you need someone to write code, lead a team, or help you look credible before a fundraising round—I can help.
